About Us

Our mission is to support mental health and wellbeing through accessible resources an proffessional support.Therapy Aura was founded to remove the traditional barriers to therapy and make mental health care more accessible to everyone and in any language. Today, it is providing professional, affordable, and personalized therapy in a convenient online format.Therapy Aura network of over 30,000 licensed therapists to help millions of people take ownership of their mental health and work towards their personal goals.Therapy Aura is committed to expanding access to therapy globally.
